What Are We Aiming To Become ?

What Is Mentoring?

Piyush knows Competitive Coding and wants to learn Web Development. The best for him will be that he gets access to structured curriculum. And a mentor for one on one guidance and doubt clearing sessions will be cherry over cake for him. Here comes Edloops in play, Piyush can get both of them here. In return he just needs to mentor someone who wants to learn Competitive Coding

Then how this Share of skills will take place?

Whenever Piyush is mentoring someone on Edloops he will earn Edcoins, these Edcoins can be redeemed by him to learn another skill and get mentored by mentor of his choice. He will get access to full statistics and roadmap of his learning from the Machine Learning Models of Edloops, so that he can stay on top through out the course.

Does mentoring others to learn in return sounds
too much of work to you?

Well by Mentoring others, you can strengthen and deepen your understanding of a particular skill, and also remain in touch with skills while developing more. Personal Growth: By mentoring and being mentored, you can gain a better understanding of yourself and identify areas for personal growth and development. Improved Communication Skills: Mentoring requires clear and effective communication, which can help you improve your communication skills, both professionally and personally.
